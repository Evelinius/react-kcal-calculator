I usually take breakfast and lunch at Mealty and I'm really sick of counting calories, prots, fats and carbs by myself. So I developed a program that calculates the daily cals/prots/fats/carbs intake based on your parameters and takes this rate as a basis, then you can see how much a particular dish fills the norm of your macronutrients.

How it works

Enter your parameters, chose your goal

Then chose the dish. I pulled the menu from the Mealty's site using a script and converted it to JSON. It contains photos, names, number of calories, and so on.

You can choose as many as you want. Each dish is allocated its own random color and it takes its place in the progress bars
